Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This policy is written for a Google Slides add-on that translates slide content between Arabic and English. It is intended to clearly describe what data is processed, why, and how it is protected.

1) What data we process

  • Slide content you select to translate (text in shapes, tables, speaker notes if enabled).
  • Technical metadata needed to operate (e.g., request identifiers, timestamps, error codes).

2) What data we do not sell

We do not sell your data, and we do not use your content for advertising.

3) How translation is performed

The add-on may send text to a translation service/provider to return the translated output. The specific provider depends on your deployment configuration (e.g., Google Translate or another AI provider).

4) Data retention

  • Default: no persistent storage of slide content.
  • Logs: operational logs may be retained for troubleshooting for a limited period, and should be redacted where feasible.

5) Access and permissions

The add-on requests only the minimum Google Workspace permissions required to read slide content and write translated output. Access is scoped to the document(s) where you run the add-on.

6) Security controls

  • Transport security (HTTPS) for any network communications.
  • Role-based access and least privilege for operational accounts.
  • Monitoring and incident response procedures for abnormal behavior.

7) International transfers

Depending on your translation provider and hosting region, data may be processed outside your country. You should configure providers/regions to meet your organization’s compliance requirements.

8) Contact

Privacy inquiries: privacy@yourdomain.tld

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the add-on or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
